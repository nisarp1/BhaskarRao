IPS Career

From Enforcement to Empowerment

A decorated officer of the Indian Police Service, Bhaskar Rao has led critical divisions including the Internal Security Division, Railways, and the Bengaluru City Police. Known as the "People's Commissioner," he is now dedicated to public service through the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP).

  • President's Medal for Distinguished Service (2015).
